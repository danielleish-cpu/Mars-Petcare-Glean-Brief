The Context Challenge

When R&D knowledge is fragmented, science moves slower than it should.

In regulated, evidence-heavy businesses like pet health and nutrition, teams typically rely on PLM/recipe systems, ELNs, LIMS, SharePoint libraries, regulatory dossiers, and vendor files. That fragmentation creates friction in exactly the workflows that matter most: formulation, claim development, regulatory defense, vet channel translation, and talent onboarding.

The challenge is structural, not one of effort. And at Mars Petcare's scale and ambition, the cost of that friction compounds quickly.

What Glean Provides

An enterprise context layer to let AI understand your unique business.

Glean is a secure, permission-aware platform that connects to existing systems and builds a governed context graph across them. It understands the relationships between studies, formulations, indications, and outcomes, and lets teams interact with that context through search, chat, and task-specific agents.

Critically, Glean does not store or move data. It creates a context layer on top of your existing infrastructure, with real-time permissions inherited from every connected system.

Glean is designed to complement, not compete with, existing AI investments. It provides the trusted scientific context layer that makes every other AI initiative, from predictive diagnostics to consumer-facing experiences, more effective and safer.
Strategic Value in Hard Dollars

Where context translates to outcomes.

In comparable R&D-intensive, regulated environments, we see the same patterns. Below is how context typically converts to measurable returns, mapped to outcomes that matter in a therapeutic health nutrition business.

🚀

Faster Formulation and Portfolio Cycles

When scientists can ask in natural language, "Show prior trials with [ingredient X] in feline renal diets, including dose ranges and digestibility outcomes," and get permission-aware results across PLM, ELN, and historical studies in seconds, the weeks spent on manual evidence archaeology collapse.

30-50% reduction in evidence gathering time
🛡️

Stronger Claims and Regulatory Defensibility

Every therapeutic diet depends on its evidence package. Glean agents can assemble structured evidence summaries, draft micro-claims tied to specific studies, and flag prior regulatory pushback, all with full citation chains. For organizations expanding access through omnichannel while protecting medical integrity, that traceability is foundational.

Evidence package assembly: days to hours
🔬

Reduced Duplicated R&D Spend

With decades of research across species, geographies, and brands, it is common for new scientists or regional teams to re-run studies that exist under a different name or protocol. Similarity search lets a researcher paste a draft protocol and instantly find "near neighbors," redirecting budget toward genuinely novel work.

Industry benchmark: 15-20% of R&D effort is duplicative
🏥

Science-to-Channel Translation

R&D findings typically need to flow into vet detailing, clinical protocols, and consumer-facing digital tools. Today, that translation tends to be manual. Glean agents can generate vet-ready clinical guidance, training materials, and validated content, all governed so only vetted science reaches external audiences.

Single governed source of truth across all channels
👥

Accelerated R&D and Digital Talent Onboarding

New scientists, data scientists, and digital hires in complex R&D environments typically spend months learning where data lives and what the organization already knows. Glean acts as a day-one, role-aware knowledge assistant embedded in the tools people already use.

50%+ faster time-to-self-sufficiency (peer benchmark)

Why a Context Layer Matters

Context is the differentiator.

Most AI tools generate answers. Few can trace every answer back to its source, respect document-level permissions, and model the relationships between entities in a complex R&D environment. That is the gap Glean fills as an enterprise context layer.

Horizontal, Not Point

Connects once to PLM, ELN, LIMS, SharePoint, Teams, email, and clinical platforms. Every AI experience draws from the same governed context.

Built for Regulated Domains

Enterprise Context Graph
